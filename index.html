Inn Alex Holiday Units

Located in Alex’s Golden Triangle (the elevated part of the headland between Buderim Rd and Pacific Tce with views of the beaches to the north)... these meticulously designed and built Home Units are only 3 minutes’ walk to the beach. This location is what people love. North facing, with glimpses of the surf from Unit 1, and from Unit 2 beautiful views of the beaches, mountains, headlands, river mouth, and Old Woman Island.
